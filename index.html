Sayeret Givati

Sayret Givati is the spearhead of Givati bridge in the IDF. The soldiers' training begins with a three-day training process.
After that the soldiers begin the training, which lasts about a year and two months.
During the training, the soldiers will spend weeks firing, fighting in a built-up and open area, as well as the following courses:

  • Counter-terrorism
  • Underground warfare
  • Intelligence and patrol
